About

Teachers waste trillions of pounds of paper everyday printing syllabi for their students to take home and have their parents sign. Sign My Syllabus aims to eliminate this waste. Teachers may simply upload the documents requiring parental review and parents can sign them using DocuSign's patented electronic signature service.
